Add Category Entry
1) Click the “AddNew” button.Then you get the following screen:
2) Enter the Fish Type.
3) Click the Add button.You will be prompted for a confirmation.Click on the Ok button.The newly added entry will be visible on the Fish Category list
Edit Category Entry
1)Click the "Edit" button .Then you get the following screen:
2) You can edit the Fish Type.
3) Click on the "Save Changes" button.You will be prompted for confirmation.Click on Ok button.The changes will be visible in the Fish Category list.
Delete Category Entry
1) You can select more than one entries by selecting the checkboxes for deletion.
2) Click the Delete button.You will be prompted for confirmation.(as shown below):
Click on the Ok buton.The selected entry(entries) will be removed from the Fish Category list.
