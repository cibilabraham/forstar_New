Add Landing Center Entry
1) Click the “AddNew” button.Then you get the following screen:
2) Enter the Landing center code . Eg:You can use KL for KERALA.
3) Enter the Landing center name.
4) The description field is optional.
5) Click the Add button.You will be prompted for a confirmation.Click on the Ok button.The newly added entry will be visible in the LandingCenter list.
Edit Landing Center Entry
1)Click the "Edit" button .Then you get the following screen:
2) You can edit the landing center code,name and/or description.
3) Click on the "Save Changes" button.You will be prompted for confirmation.Click on the Ok button.The changes will be visible in the LandingCenter list.
Delete Landing Center Entry
1) You can select more than one entries by selecting the checkboxes against each entry.
2) Click the Delete button.You will be prompted for confirmation.(as shown below):
Click on the Ok buton.The selected entry(entries) will be removed from the LandingCenter list.
