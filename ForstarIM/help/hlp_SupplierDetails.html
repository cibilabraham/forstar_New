1)Add Supplier Entry
a) Click the “AddNew” button.Then you get the following screen:
b) Enter the Code for supplier .
c) Enter the Supplier Name.
d) Select the Place .
e) Select the Landing Center.You can select multiple Landing Center .
f) Enter the Telephone Number .
g)Address,Pin Code,Fax No,Email,PAN No are optional.
f) Click the Add button.You will be prompted for a confirmation.Click on the Ok button.The newly added entry will be visible in the Suppliers Master list.
2)Edit Supplier Entry
a)Click on the "Edit" button against each supplier entry, you will get editable screen with subsupplier list of corresponding supplier as shown in the figure.
b) You can edit the supplier Code,Name,Telephone Number,Address,Place,Pin Code,Fax No,Email and/or PAN No .
c) Click on the "Save Changes" button.You will be prompted for confirmation.Click on the Ok button.The changes will be visible in the Suppliers Master list.
2.1)Sub Supplier List
2.2)Add Sub Supplier Entry
a) Click the “AddNew” button.Then you get the following screen:
b) Enter the Code for Sub Supplier .
c) Enter the Sub Supplier Name.
d) Select the Place .
e) Enter the Telephone Number .
f)Address,Pin Code,Fax No,Email,LST No,CST No,PAN No are optional.
f) Click the Add button.You will be prompted for a confirmation.Click on the Ok button.The newly added entry will be visible in the Sub Suppliers list.
2.3)Edit Sub Supplier Entry
a)Click the "Edit" button .Then you get the following screen:
b) You can edit the Sub Supplier Code,Name,Address,Telephone Number,Place,Pin Code,Fax No,Email,LST No,CST No and/or PAN No .
c) Click on the "Save Changes" button.You will be prompted for confirmation.Click on the Ok button.The changes will be visible in the Sub Suppliers list.
